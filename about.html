About Sharda Library

Sharda Library is more than just a place to read — it’s a community hub where knowledge, comfort, and modern facilities come together. Inspired by the Little Free Library movement, our mission is to make learning accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

With features like free WiFi, air‑conditioned study rooms, secure lockers, CCTV monitoring, and affordable membership fees, we provide a safe and supportive environment for students, professionals, and lifelong learners.
