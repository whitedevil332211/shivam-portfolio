About Us

Sharda Library is a modern learning hub designed to provide students and professionals with a peaceful environment for study and research. With state-of-the-art facilities, free WiFi, AC rooms, and a wide collection of resources, we ensure that every visitor finds the perfect atmosphere to focus and grow.

Our mission is to build a community of learners by offering affordable membership, daily newspapers, secure lockers, and a safe environment with CCTV surveillance. Sharda Library is not just a place to read — it’s a place to belong.
