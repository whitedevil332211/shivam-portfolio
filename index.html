Our Features

Feature 1 (WiFi)

The **WiFi facility at Sharda Library** is one of its most valuable features. It provides members with fast, reliable, and secure internet access throughout the library space. Whether you’re working on research projects, attending online classes, or simply browsing for information, the high‑speed connection ensures that your work is uninterrupted. This service is especially helpful for students and professionals who bring their laptops or mobile devices, as it allows them to combine traditional resources like books with modern digital tools. The WiFi is available across all study areas, so you can choose your favorite corner and stay connected while working in a peaceful environment. In short, the WiFi facility makes Sharda Library a modern learning hub, blending the quiet focus of a traditional library with the convenience of digital access.

Feature 2 - AC Room

The **AC room facility at Sharda Library** is designed to make studying and reading more comfortable, especially during the hot summer months. With well‑maintained air conditioning, members can focus on their work without worrying about the heat or humidity. This facility ensures a calm and pleasant environment where students, professionals, and community members can spend long hours productively. The cool and quiet atmosphere not only helps with concentration but also makes group discussions and study sessions more enjoyable. By providing an air‑conditioned space, Sharda Library shows its commitment to creating a modern, supportive learning hub that values both comfort and productivity. It’s the perfect blend of traditional library resources with the convenience of modern amenities.

Feature 3 (Best place for study)

The **“Best Place for Study” facility at Sharda Library** is all about creating the perfect environment for focus and productivity. The library offers comfortable seating, quiet surroundings, and well‑lit study areas that help learners stay engaged for long hours. Students preparing for exams, professionals working on projects, or anyone who simply wants a peaceful corner to read will find the atmosphere supportive and motivating. The combination of silence, air‑conditioned rooms, and free WiFi makes it easy to concentrate without distractions. By blending modern amenities with a calm, traditional library setting, Sharda Library truly becomes the best place for study — a space where knowledge, comfort, and community come together to help you achieve your goals.

Feature 4 - free news paper

The **Free Newspaper facility at Sharda Library** is a thoughtful addition that keeps readers informed and connected with the world. Every day, members can access leading newspapers without any extra cost, making it easy to stay updated on current affairs, educational news, and community happenings. This service is especially useful for students preparing for competitive exams, as newspapers provide valuable insights into general knowledge, current events, and language skills. For casual readers, it’s a great way to enjoy morning reading in a peaceful environment. By offering free newspapers, Sharda Library ensures that knowledge isn’t limited to books alone — it extends to daily updates, opinions, and stories that shape our society. It’s another way the library supports learning, awareness, and community engagement.

Feature 5 - lockers availability

The **locker facility at Sharda Library** is provided to ensure that members can study in comfort without worrying about their personal belongings. Secure lockers are available for students and visitors to safely store bags, books, laptops, or other items while they focus on reading and learning. This service is especially useful for those who spend long hours in the library, as it allows them to keep their essentials safe and organized. Each locker is easy to use, spacious enough for study materials, and maintained regularly for cleanliness and security. By offering lockers, Sharda Library creates a stress‑free environment where members can concentrate fully on their studies, knowing their belongings are protected. It’s another way the library combines convenience with care for its community.

Feature 6 - cctv availability for security

The **CCTV facility at Sharda Library** is an important feature that ensures safety and security for all members. With surveillance cameras installed across key areas of the library, visitors can study and read with peace of mind knowing that their environment is being monitored. This system helps protect personal belongings, maintain discipline, and create a safe atmosphere for students, professionals, and community members. The presence of CCTV also supports the library staff in managing the space efficiently and responding quickly if any issue arises. By offering CCTV availability, Sharda Library shows its commitment to providing not only a comfortable study environment but also a secure one — so that every visitor can focus on learning without worry.

Feature 7 - affordable fees

The **affordable fees at Sharda Library** make it accessible to everyone in the community. By keeping membership costs low, the library ensures that students, professionals, and avid readers can enjoy all its facilities — from WiFi and AC rooms to newspapers and secure lockers — without financial burden. This approach reflects the library’s mission to promote learning and inclusivity. Affordable fees mean that education and resources are not limited to a few, but open to all who wish to grow, study, and connect. It’s a way of saying that knowledge should never be out of reach because of cost. In short, Sharda Library combines modern amenities with community care, offering a high‑quality study environment at a price that everyone can manage.

Feature 8 - power backup

The **power backup facility at Sharda Library** ensures that learning never stops, even during unexpected electricity cuts. With reliable backup systems in place, the library maintains uninterrupted lighting, air conditioning, and WiFi so that members can continue studying without disruption. This feature is especially important for students preparing for exams or professionals working on deadlines, as it guarantees a consistent and comfortable environment. The presence of power backup reflects the library’s commitment to providing a dependable study space where focus and productivity are never compromised. In short, Sharda Library’s power backup facility makes it a truly resilient and modern hub for learning, giving visitors peace of mind that their study time will always be protected.
