Creative Corner

“Code ki duniya mein jo kho jaata hai,
Bugs ke jungle mein bhi raasta paata hai.”

This section reflects my passion for blending technology with creativity. I often explain complex programming concepts using bilingual storytelling (English + Hindi) and poetic analogies. My creative work includes Hindi shayari about coding and mythological parallels where characters from epics like the Mahabharata are reimagined as modern system components.

Creativity is not separate from technology — it’s the bridge that makes technical knowledge accessible, memorable, and engaging.
